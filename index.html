Incredible Ramtek

RAMTEK

Legend has it that Lord Rama passed this way during his period of exile. There are temples dedicated to Rama and Lakshmana. At the foot of the hill is a charming inland lake Ramsagar. The famous Sanskrit poet Kalidasa wrote his epic 'Meghdoota' here. Ramtek is a fine confluence of devotion, literature and history.
